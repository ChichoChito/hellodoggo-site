Questions

Everything you need to know.

Is Hello Doggo insured?+

Yes. Every walk and boarding stay is covered by Hello Doggo Booking Protection, which includes vet costs and property damage during active bookings.

How do I know my dog is safe?+

Every walker is background checked and personally vetted. You get real-time GPS tracking and photo updates during every walk.

How does booking work?+

Pick a service, choose a walker or let us match you, and confirm. Payments are handled securely through Stripe.

What if I need to cancel?+

Just let us know. We're flexible and we'll work it out. No hidden fees or penalties.

Can I request the same walker every time?+

Yes. Mark any walker as preferred and they'll be your default for future bookings. Your dog will know them by name.

How does "every booking feeds a rescue dog" work?+

Every completed booking triggers a donation to feed a rescue dog at an LA shelter. We post updates on our Instagram.

Can I meet my walker first?+

Of course. We offer a free meet and greet before your first booking. Totally optional but highly recommended for boarding.

What areas do you serve?+

We're launching in Northeast LA covering Silver Lake, Echo Park, Highland Park, Eagle Rock, Glassell Park, and surrounding neighborhoods. Enter your ZIP above to check.
