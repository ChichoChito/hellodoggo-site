Who we are

A neighbor who loves your dog like their own.

Hello Doggo started because we were tired of handing our dog to strangers from an app. So we became the person we wished existed. A real neighbor who knows your dog's name, their favorite sniffing spots, and what makes them do the full-body wiggle at the door.

We walk the same streets your dog loves, grab coffee at the same spots you do, and wave at the same neighbors. Hello Doggo grew out of our own neighborhood because the best care comes from someone who's already part of the community.

Every walk comes with real photos and a personal update because you deserve to know your dog had an amazing time. Not a stock photo from a stranger. A real moment from someone who genuinely loves your dog.

Questions

Everything you need to know.

Is Hello Doggo insured? +

Yes. Every walk and boarding stay is fully covered.

How do I know my dog is safe? +

Every walker goes through a background check and vetting process before they're approved. You'll get real photo updates during every walk so you always know how things are going.

How does booking work? +

Enter your ZIP code, pick a service and date, and drop your phone number. We'll text you to confirm the details and get you set up.

How do I pay? +

Payments are handled securely through Stripe. Your card info is saved after your first booking so rebooking takes seconds.

What if I need to cancel? +

Just text us. We're flexible and we'll work it out.

What areas do you serve? +

We currently serve neighborhoods across Los Angeles. Enter your ZIP code in the booking section to check if we're in your area.

What does the 5% giving pledge mean? +

Every month we donate 5% of all revenue to a rotating LA dog rescue. We post the exact amount publicly on our Instagram.

Can I request the same walker? +

Yes. After your first booking you can mark any walker as a preferred provider and they'll be your default for future walks.

What happens during a walk? +

Your walker picks up your dog, takes them on a 30-minute neighborhood walk, sends you real photos along the way, and writes a personal update when they're done.

What if my dog has special needs? +

Just let us know when you book. We'll match you with a walker experienced with your dog's specific needs.

Can I book same day? +

Yes. We offer on-demand walks when walkers are available in your area.

I have multiple dogs. Can you walk them together? +

Absolutely. Let us know how many dogs when you book and we'll price it accordingly.

Want to meet your walker first? +

No problem. Just let us know and we'll set up a free meet and greet before your first booking. Totally optional.
