Questions

Good questions, real answers.

What is Hello Doggo Booking Protection?+

Every booking through Hello Doggo includes Booking Protection, marketplace coverage for vet costs if your dog is injured during an active booking. It's built into every walk, drop-in, and boarding stay automatically.

How do I know my dog is safe?+

We're background checked and we're your actual neighbors, not strangers dispatched from another zip code. You get photo updates during every walk and a full report when we're done.

Can we meet first?+

Yes, and we love doing it. Free meet and greet before your first booking. No strings attached. Especially recommended before a boarding stay.

What if I need to cancel?+

We ask for at least 24 hours notice so we can open the slot for someone else. Cancel with enough notice and there's no charge. Last-minute cancellations may be billed at the full rate.

Can I book the same person every time?+

That's the whole point. Consistency matters. Your dog should know who's coming to the door. Just let us know and we'll make it the same person every time.

How does the rescue dog donation work?+

Every completed booking triggers a $0.50 donation to an LA rescue org. We rotate partners monthly and post updates on Instagram so you see exactly where it goes.

Do you do recurring bookings?+

Absolutely. Most of our clients are on a regular schedule. Same walker, same time, same route. Tell us what works and we'll lock it in.

What neighborhoods do you cover?+

We're based in Glassell Park and cover surrounding NELA: Silver Lake, Echo Park, Highland Park, Eagle Rock, Atwater Village, Mount Washington, Glendale, and Pasadena. Not sure if you're in range? Just message us.
